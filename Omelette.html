Homemade Omelette

Recipe and Image from Joy Food Sunshine

Ingredients (1 Serving)

  • 1½ Tablespoon salted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • Pinch salt and pepper
  • ¼ cup sharp cheddar cheese shredded

Fillings (select by preference)

    Vegetables

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup vegetables diced small (bell pepper, zucchini, onion, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Onion Bacon, Garlic

  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 Tablespoon minced onion
  • 2 slices bacon diced

Instructions

  1. Prepare Filling
    • Dice 1 cup of vegetables.
    • Over medium-high heat, sauté the vegetables and garlic in olive oil until soft, about 3-5 minutes depending on the vegetables you choose. Set aside.
    • Cook bacon (or sausage), garlic and onion over medium heat until bacon is cooked and onion is soft (5-8 minutes). Set aside.
  2. Whisk eggs vigorously until they are homogenous (uniform throughout). Whisk in salt and pepper.
  3. Melt butter in an 8” nonstick skillet over medium-low heat.
  4. Add whisked eggs and swirl the pan until they are evenly distributed.
  5. Cover the pan and cook over medium-low heat until the eggs begin to set. Once they begin to set, use a spatula to gently push the edges in slightly and swirl the pan so that any uncooked eggs on the top pour over the sides to the edges of the pan and cook.
  6. Once the omelette is mostly set (about 5-6 minutes), carefully flip it and turn off the heat.
  7. Sprinkle cheese over the top of the omelette.
  8. Add the toppings on one half of the omelette, fold the other half over the toppings, and serve.
